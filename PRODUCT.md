# Salone Product Context

Salone is a salon and stylist marketplace for customers, independent stylists, salon-exclusive stylists, and salon admins.

## Users

- Customer: finds services, books appointments, receives booking/reschedule updates.
- Stylist/master: manages bookings, services, pricing, home service availability, and reschedules.
- Salon admin: manages salon bookings and staff permissions.

## MVP Priorities

1. Stable booking request flow.
2. Reschedule approval flow between customer and provider.
3. Service and price management.
4. Admin booking actions.
5. Availability and queue management.
6. Notifications.
7. Authentication.
8. Payments and payouts.

## Non-goals For Now

- Do not add payment provider until booking flow is stable.
- Do not add large agent frameworks into the app.
- Do not redesign every screen at once.
- Do not replace the backend or Flutter apps.
