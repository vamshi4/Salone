## Review Checklist

Before approval:

- Is the claim true in the live app or current Play build?
- Is the language simple enough for salon owners?
- Does it avoid sharing private/customer data?
- Does it avoid false revenue promises?
- Is the CTA clear?
- Does the visual match Chairful branding?
