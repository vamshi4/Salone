# Marketing Asset Guide

This guide defines how Chairful creates photos, videos, screenshots, and generated assets for social media.

## Asset Types

### Product Screenshot

Use when:
- The feature is visible in the app.
- We want to build trust.
- The UI is polished enough for public viewing.

Required:
- Hide real phone numbers and customer names.
- Prefer test/demo salon data.
- Use latest Play/internal build when possible.

### Demo Video

Use when:
- The workflow is better shown as steps.
- The feature is interactive.

Good examples:
- Add a walk-in service
- View daily earnings
- Book a repeat customer
- Add staff and services

Length:
- 10-20 seconds for reels/shorts
- 30-60 seconds for product walkthrough

### Generated Image

Use when:
- We need a clean visual concept.
- No real salon asset exists.
- The post is about a pain point, not a UI workflow.

Rules:
- Make visuals look Indian SMB salon relevant.
- Avoid luxury-only salon imagery.
- Avoid fake celebrity/customer images.
- Avoid showing private data.

### Short Reel

Use when:
- One pain point can be shown quickly.
- The message is simple.

Structure:

1. Hook: "Do you know who stopped coming to your salon?"
2. Problem: "Most owners remember only today's customers."
3. Product moment: show Chairful list/report.
4. CTA: "Try Chairful with your salon team."

## Brand Direction

Chairful should feel:

- Simple
- Trustworthy
- Local
- Useful
- Business-focused

Avoid:

- Overly glossy startup visuals
- Purple-blue generic SaaS gradients
- Fake luxury salon stock style
- Too much text in one image
