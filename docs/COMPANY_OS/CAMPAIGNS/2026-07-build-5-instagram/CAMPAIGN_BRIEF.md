# Campaign Brief - Build 5 Instagram

Status: Draft
Channel: Instagram only
Release source: Build 5 (`Chairful-2.0.0-b5`) + prod backend verification
Primary theme: Stop losing walk-in money
Risk level: Low, if we avoid revenue guarantees and use demo/test data only.

## What Changed

Build 5 adds owner-facing tools that are easy to explain visually:

- Done service / walk-in logging
- Daily, weekly, and monthly earnings view
- Customer autocomplete for repeat customers
- Cleaner darker UI and clearer booking actions

## Who It Helps

Small Indian salon owners who still track walk-ins, staff work, and daily income from memory, paper, or WhatsApp chats.

## Salon Owner Pain

Many walk-ins are completed quickly and forgotten later. If the owner does not log them, daily earnings become unclear, repeat customers are harder to remember, and the salon loses control of its own data.

## Plain-English Benefit

Chairful helps salon owners record finished walk-ins, see earnings clearly, and book repeat customers faster.

## Campaign Positioning

Do not present Chairful as "another booking app." Present it as:

> A simple salon management app that helps owners track every service and know what happened today.

## Instagram Content Goals

1. Make salon owners feel the pain of untracked walk-ins.
2. Show Chairful as simple and practical.
3. Get DMs from early salon owners.
4. Build proof before larger launch.

## Audience

- Independent salon owners
- Small salon teams with 1-8 staff
- Owners using WhatsApp/notebooks for tracking
- India-first, phone-first, practical users
