# Instagram Reel Scripts - Build 5

## Reel 1 - The Forgotten Walk-In

Status: Draft
Length: 12-18 seconds
Feature/source: Done service / walk-in logging

### Hook

Text on screen:

`You finished the service... but forgot to record it?`

### Scene Plan

1. Owner at counter after a busy day.
   - Text: "Busy salon day"
2. Owner trying to remember walk-ins.
   - Text: "Was it 8 or 11 walk-ins?"
3. Phone screen / app demo:
   - Tap "Done service"
   - Select service
   - Save completed service
4. End card:
   - Text: "Chairful counts every completed service"

### Voiceover

"Walk-ins happen fast. But if you forget to record them, your daily earnings become guesswork. Chairful helps you log completed services in one step."

### Caption

Do not let walk-ins disappear from your daily numbers.

Chairful helps salon owners record completed services and keep earnings clear.
